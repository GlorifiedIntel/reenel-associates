About Us

Reenel Associates is a personnel development company delivering unique and well-researched programmes in the areas of Capacity Building, Training, Retreat Management, Conferences, Empowerment, Outsourcing, logistics, Exhibitions and consultancy services for public and private sector reform.

We work with both public and private sectors in Nigeria, Ghana, Kigali, South Africa, Europe, Middle East and Latin America, as well as for international development agencies and institutions.

As a functional market intelligence expert, we have recorded successes in repackaging the human capital resources of many organizations and have carved a niche in the marketplace. Reenel Associates has established and developed the human capital resources and operational procedures of many organizations all of which are still in functional businesses today.

The principal promoter of Reenel Associates is a certified SME toolkit trainer by IFC, an Arm of the World Bank, a full member of the Nigerian institute of training and Development, fellow Institute of Professional Managers & Administrators of Nigeria & corporate member Institute of Information Management Africa. We are confident that our proposed partnership with your organization will create a pragmatic impact on our workforce and ultimately bring better performance and productivity.

Our Brand Driver

At Reenel Associates, we believe that living by a set of core values help define the true measure of a company; they guide the way we treat each other and how we make business decisions, regardless of time, situation or person. They are the very essence of our company’s culture; the soul of our company. Ours is about building an organisation that has purpose, focus and alignment, which lives its values every day and creates an environment that allows employees to grow and to produce superior results.
