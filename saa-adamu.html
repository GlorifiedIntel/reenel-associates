Our Team

Saa Adamu

Dr. Sa’adatu Adamu is an accomplished mental health counselor with extensive experience working with victims of human trafficking, rape, substance abuse, and emotional trauma. Her academic journey began with a degree in Industrial Design and Motion Pictures from the National Film Institute, an affiliate of Abubakar Tafawa Balewa University (ATBU), Bauchi. She holds a Postgraduate Diploma in Education (PGDE), a Master of Science (MSc), and a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Educational Guidance and Counseling from Nasarawa State University, Nigeria (NSUK). She was also conferred with an honorary PhD in Management and Leadership by the American Management University and holds a certificate in Human Resources Management from Unicaf University, Cyprus.

Dr. Adamu brings a unique blend of high-tech training and exceptional counseling skills, combined with expertise in startup business consulting, mental health, project development, strategic planning, mentorship, and research. Her career began at NAFDAC Green Pages, where she served as Client Service Executive and Zonal Coordinator, Abuja. In 2014, she transitioned to lead Maisa Legado Training Consultancy Firm as its Head.

Dr. Adamu is a recognized thought leader in mental health and counseling, having attended and organized numerous international conferences, workshops, and seminars addressing human trafficking, mental health, and counseling. She is the author of two impactful books, *Break the Silence* and *The Voices in Me*, both of which advocate for mental health awareness among secondary school students. Her research work includes publications in reputable journals, including the *International Journal of Research and Scientific Innovation*.

In 2021, Dr. Adamu facilitated a gender-based violence (GBV) awareness project for primary and secondary students in urban and rural schools under the Spotlight Initiative, sponsored by the UN and EU through UNESCO. She also facilitated the Concord Initiative's mental health and psychosocial support training, organized by OSPRE and sponsored by GIZ. Since 2018, her advocacy has focused on school campaigns, in partnership with the Citizens Commission on Human Rights (CCHR). She inaugurated Nigeria’s first mental health club in partnership with the Secondary Education Board, launching at GSS Wuye to address rising mental health challenges in FCT secondary schools.

Dr. Adamu is the Founder of SDF Rehabilitation Home, President of the Psychotherapeutic & Counseling Research Institute, and CEO of Maisa Legado Limited. She is a member of the National Youth Policy Review Committee, inaugurated by the Minister of Youth Development. Her contributions have earned her numerous awards, and she serves on various boards. Dr. Adamu is a Fellow of the Centre for Public Service Productivity and Development (CEPROD) and the National Institute of Credit Administration (NICA). She currently serves as the Vice Chairperson of the Counseling Association of Nigeria (CASSON) FCT Chapter and is a member of esteemed organizations, including the American Counseling Association (ACA), International Society of Substance Use Professionals (ISSUP), Global Mental Health Action Network (GMHAN), and the World Drug Federation (WDF).
