Our Team

Annie Goerge

Dr. Aniefiok George is a distinguished professional with extensive expertise in policy development, strategic planning, and international relations. Driven by a strong commitment to fostering global understanding and collaboration, she has built a remarkable career across multiple sectors, including oil and gas, education, and gender advocacy.

Her professional journey encompasses diverse roles in human relations, public relations, and administrative functions. Notably, she served with the Government of Akwa Ibom State in the Office of the Secretary to the State, focusing on administrative matters, youth education, and women empowerment initiatives.

Dr. George holds a Bachelor’s degree in Geology, a Master of Business Administration (MBA), a Postgraduate Diploma (PGD) in Public Relations and Advertising, as well as a Master’s degree and a Doctorate (PhD) in Gender Studies and Youth Development. Known for her exceptional leadership qualities, analytical and organizational skills, she is a self-motivated team player and mobilizer who thrives in tackling challenging tasks. Her passion for continuous learning and personal growth underscores her professional ethos.

An ardent advocate for youth development, girl-child education, and women empowerment, Dr. George has conducted impactful training sessions addressing critical issues such as the adverse effects of early marriage, teenage pregnancy in Northern Nigeria, and the importance of self-actualization for girls. As the Founder and CEO of Food 4 Life Enterprise, she continues to inspire and empower women and young people through innovative initiatives and transformative leadership.
